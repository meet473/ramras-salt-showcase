Culinary Wellness

Cook With RAMRAS Pink Salt

For the most part, you can cook and season with Himalayan pink salt like you would regular table salt. You can add it to marinades and sauces, or wait until the food is on the table to season it.

There are a few considerations for cooking. If the salt is coarse, you might need more of it to match the salty flavor of ground salt.

Plus, Himalayan pink salt features less sodium chloride and no artificial anti-caking agents unlike traditional processed table salt. With less sodium chloride, you can focus on flavor without worrying about excessive sodium intake.
